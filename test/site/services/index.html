Cosmetic Bleaching

Teeth can now be whitened by cosmetic bleaching. Impressions of the teeth are taken to make a customized tray, which is then coated with a gel. The tray is worn for several hours or overnight. Teeth typically lighten in 10-14 days.

All Porcelain Crowns

Crowns can now be made for front or back teeth which are made of casted porcelain. Because they do not have a metal understructure like traditional porcelain crowns, light transmits through the crown giving it an appearance which is difficult to distinguish from natural teeth.

Dental Implants

It is now possible to place dental implants in upper and lower jaws so that dental restorations can be built on those implants. Implants are especially effective for the replacement of a missing front or back tooth as well as making dentures more secure.

Snoring/Sleep Apnea Appliances

Specialized appliances can now be made to help patients with snoring or sleep apnea. These appliances are easy to make and have shown to be a conservative and cost-effective way to manage these problems.

Adult Orthodontics

Adults who wish to enhance their appearance may opt for orthodontic therapy. Patients who require only minor tooth movement may qualify for this treatment which uses clear, removable, trays are used to move teeth.

Special Patient Dentistry

Our office is happy to treat patients with special needs. Medically compromised patients with one or more serious medical problems may require specific modifications to their dental treatment. Our office is wheelchair accessible and complies with Americans with Disabilities Act regulations.
