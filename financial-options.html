Financial Options

Making Payments and Insurance Coverage

We accept cash, personal checks, money orders, major credit cards, FSA cards and HSA cards. Payment is typically due at the time of service, but we do make financial arrangements for more extensive services.

We also accept CareCredit, a healthcare credit card with a credit line for treatments and procedures for your entire family. If you have insurance, please bring your insurance card with you. We submit to all insurance companies but may not be an in network provider for your plan. We do not participate in any HMO, DMO or discount plans.
